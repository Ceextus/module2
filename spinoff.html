Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Spaghetti400g
Eggs4 large
Pancetta200g
Parmesan Cheese50g, grated
Black Pepperto taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan.
  3. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy, then remove from heat.
  4. Combine the pasta with pancetta and egg mixture, tossing to coat evenly.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

Recipe source: Great Italian Chefs

Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Chicken Breast200g, grilled
Romaine Lettuce1 head
Croutons1 cup
Caesar Dressing1/2 cup
Parmesan Cheese30g, shaved

Steps

  1. Grill the chicken and slice it into strips.
  2. Chop the lettuce and mix with Caesar dressing.
  3. Top with grilled chicken, croutons, and Parmesan.
  4. Serve immediately.

Recipe source: Simply Recipes

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Pizza Dough1 round
Tomato Sauce1/2 cup
Fresh Mozzarella100g
Fresh Basilhandful
Olive Oildrizzle

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Top with fresh mozzarella and basil leaves.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and serve hot.

Recipe source: A Couple Cooks
